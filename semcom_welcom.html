The S. G. M. English Medium College of Commerce and Management

was established in the year 1997 and has completed over two decades in providing quality education to its students. The full form of the institute is Sardar Gunj Mercantile Bank Limited (Anand) English Medium College of Commerce and Management but it is popularly known as SEMCOM. Located in the state of Gujarat, the S G M English Medium College of Commerce and Management was set up by the Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM) and is affiliated to the Sardar Patel University of Vallabh Vidyanagar. The aim of S. G. M. English Medium College of Commerce and Management has been imparting world-class education to students who wish to pursue undergraduate courses in the fields of commerce, management, and information technology (IT). SEMCOM was accredited by NAAC in 2015 with an ‘A’ grade.
