Master of Commerce (M.Com.)

The two-year postgraduate degree Programme affiliated to Charutar Vidya Mandal University attempts to cover all relevant aspects of academics and commerce in a highly pragmatic manner. The classroom learning is enriched by various case studies, management games, seminar presentations, group discussions, interaction with practicing managers, e-commerce professionals, etc.

Program Outcomes

PO-1

Sound theoretical foundation helps in identification business problems and provide innovative solutions, molding them into future visionaries, management leaders.

PO-2

Research orientation for analyzing problems and formulating strategies to cope up with business opportunities.

PO-3

Competency to be employable in the global market

PO-4

Conceptual and analytical abilities required for effective decision making.

Eligibility

Candidate who has passed Bachelor's degree in Commerce / Management / Equivalent under 10+2+3
