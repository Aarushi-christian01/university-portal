Bachelor of Business Administration (Hons.)

BBA (Hons.) program offers specialization in Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Business Analytics etc. This program also offers practical exposure in terms of industrial visits and training as a part of course curriculum. In addition, the focus is on enhancing presentation skills and other managerial skills.

Program Outcomes

PO-1

Knowledge of management science to solve complex corporate problems using limited resources.

PO-2

Analytical abilities towards human capital productivity data associated with business development, growth and sustainability.

PO-2

Sustainable Business Practices Demonstrating sensitivity to social, ethical and sustainability issues.

PO-3

Reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to management practice.

Eligibility

Candidate who has completed 12th / HSC / Equivalent.
