Computer Science Department

ISTAR (Institute of Science & Technology for Advanced Studies & Research) offers various computer science and related programs.These include Master of Science (M.Sc.) degrees in Computer Application & Information Technology, and Cyber Security. ISTAR also offers a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program. The institute emphasizes hands-on experience, well-equipped labs, and strong industry connections through MoUs and placement opportunities.

Key aspects of ISTAR's Computer Science programs:

M.Sc. in Computer Application & Information Technology:

Focuses on developing skilled professionals for the IT industry, with a strong emphasis on practical application and industry standards.

M.Sc. in Cyber Security:

Aims to produce graduates with expertise in tackling real-world cyber threats and security challenges, covering areas like ethical hacking, network security, and data privacy.

MCA Program:

Provides a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and prepares students for careers in diverse IT fields.
