M.Sc. (Industrial Hygiene & Safety)

at the Institute of Science & Technology for Advanced Studies & Research (ISTAR). It's the first full-time post-graduate program of its kind in India, focusing on industrial hygiene and safety.

Key aspects of the ISTAR MIHS department:

Program:

Offers a Master of Science degree in Industrial Hygiene and Safety.

Focus:

Equips students with the knowledge and skills to identify, evaluate, and control workplace hazards to ensure safe working conditions.

Curriculum:

Includes aspects of both industrial hygiene (identifying and controlling health hazards) and safety (creating safe working environments).

Career opportunities:

Graduates can pursue careers in industrial hygiene consultancies, safety consultancies, various industries (engineering, refineries, etc.), and multinational corporations.
