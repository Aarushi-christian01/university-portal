Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

BCA program offers highly specialized knowledge pertaining to Information Technology, Software Engineering, Systems Analysis and Design. The program has tremendous laboratory inputs which enable the students to obtain expertise in the areas of Software Development, Networking, etc. The program has a unique exposure of Summer Training for System Study and Business Process Study which offers a live business environment, enhancing the learning capability in the discipline.

Program Outcomes

PO-1

Understand, Analyse, Design solutions and Develop computer programs in the areas related to structured and object-oriented programming, code algorithms, website designing and networking for efficient design of computer based system.

PO-2

Develop the potential to work in the IT sector as system engineer, software tester, Junior programmer, web developer, system administrator, software developer, database designer, systems' analyst, etc.

PO-3

Apply standard software engineering practices and strategies in software project development using open source programming environment to deliver a quality of product for business success.

PO-4

Meet the requirements of the Industrial standards and become potentially rich and employable in Indian & global software market.

Eligibility

Candidate who has completed 12th / HSC / Equivalent.
