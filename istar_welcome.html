Institute of Science & Technology for Advanced Studies & Research (ISTAR)

Vallabh Vidyanagar is ranked between 101 to 150 in NIRF ranking (2019-2020), MHRD, New Delhi. The institute has close tie-ups with Indian Research institutes and Foreign Universities as well as MoU with corporates for training and placement. The college has a record of providing 100% placement in M.Sc Industrial Chemistry and M.Sc Polymer Science & Technology. ISTAR offers postgraduate and research courses in the discipline of Industrial Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Geoinformatics, Real Estate Valuation and many more. Admissions take place on the basis of merit.
