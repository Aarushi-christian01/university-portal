Bachelor of Commerce (Hons.)

BCom (Hons.) program offers unique specializations - AccTech, CostTech, FinTech, TaxTech, which involves application of technology with basic commerce fields. This program enables the students to pursue their higher studies including professional degrees like Chartered Accountancy, Cost Accountancy, Financial Analytics etc.

Program Outcomes

PO-1

Skills-Set to apply the knowledge in various spheres of Commerce..

PO-2

Analytical and problem-solving skills within various disciplines of management, business, accounting, finance, costing and law with help of IT applications.

PO-3

in-depth learning experience in the various fields of business and also to provide a strong foundation for students to prepare for various professional courses.

PO-4

Learning experience to demonstrate relevancy of theoretical knowledge of their course to gain career related experience.

Eligibility

Candidate who has completed 12th / HSC / Equivalent.
