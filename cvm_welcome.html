THE CHARUTAR VIDYA MANDAL UNIVERSITY (CVM University, Estd. 2019), Vallabh Vidyanagar-388120, Gujarat, India stands as a beacon of excellence in higher education, fostering academic innovation, holistic development, and societal advancement. Established with a vision to empower future generations, CVMU is a proud institution rooted in the legacy of Charutar Vidya Mandal, which has been a pioneer in education for decades.

CVM University is located in the heart of Gujarat and boasts a sprawling campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It offers a diverse range of programs including BBA, PGDM in Aviation, Hospitality & Travel Management, Global Business Management and Diploma in Aviation, Tour & Travel Management catering to the aspirations of students from all walks of life.
